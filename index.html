What do I do if I'm having trouble in class?

Talk to your teachers! They are there to help you out. The TAs also make for a great resource. Beyond that, try cornerstone and PLTL for additional tutoring and help sessions!

How do I find new friends?

Be social. There are plenty of activities on any given weekend, and class is a great place to spark up a conversation. Try study groups or join a club to meet a wide range of people.

Tips

  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. Your teachers want you to succeed and there are plenty of other resources.
  • Try new things. College is a time for branching out. You will never know if you are good at, or like something unless you try.
  • Keep your options open. Don't burn bridges, you are young and your interests change. Try different classes and make sure you enjoy a subject before declaring a major.
  • Have fun. College is difficult, and Wash U can be stressful, but don't forget that it should be fun too. Get out there and make friends!
